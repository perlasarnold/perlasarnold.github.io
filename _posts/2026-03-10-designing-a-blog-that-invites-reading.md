---
title: Designing a blog that invites reading
tags:
  - Design
  - Writing
---
The best technical blogs feel calm before they feel clever.

That usually comes from structure more than decoration: a clear publishing date, a readable headline,
an excerpt that earns the click, and enough spacing that scanning the page feels natural. A good blog
homepage should help a returning reader decide in seconds what changed since their last visit.

For a GitHub Pages setup, that means leaning into a layout that is dependable. Posts should be easy to
publish in Markdown, archives should stay organized automatically, and the design should stay polished
without demanding a heavy build pipeline.
