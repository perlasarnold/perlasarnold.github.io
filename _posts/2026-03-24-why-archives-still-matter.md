---
title: Why archives still matter
tags:
  - Archive
  - Strategy
---
A strong archive changes how a site feels.

Instead of a stream where older work disappears, the archive gives your writing a longer life. Readers
can discover related topics, revisit foundational posts, and get a better sense of how your ideas are
evolving over time. That is especially important for blogs built around practical guidance.

A classic blog structure makes that longevity visible. The latest post gets the spotlight, but the rest
of the catalog never falls out of reach.
