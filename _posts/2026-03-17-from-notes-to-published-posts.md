The gap between drafting and publishing is where many blogs stall.

One useful pattern is to keep every draft moving through the same sequence: rough notes, working title,
short summary, supporting screenshots or references, and then a final pass for clarity. When the site
itself is lightweight, the publishing workflow can stay lightweight too.

That is one reason a GitHub Pages blog still works so well for focused personal publishing. The system
gets out of the way and leaves room for consistency.
